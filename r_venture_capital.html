Summary: Access to funds for fast-growing first class companies

We provide access to capital for startups in various stages with sweetspot at round A and later. We support founders in earlier stages (angel and seed), but you need to stand out.


Rounds Series A and later. We do consider exceptional angel and seed stage startups.
Focus Fast-growing businesses with global potential
Allocation Worldwide
Verticals Agnostic. *Web3: see below

NOTE: Please read the 'Initial Guidelines' carefully before submiting your deck:

  • You need to demonstrate that you understand your product and the market
  • We expect that you excel in at least one of 3Ts: Team, Traction, Technology.
  • We expect that you are solving real and existing problem.
  • Later stage startups: We expect you have clear growth or scale strategy.
  • We like shorter straight to the point decks rather than longer ones.
  • Looking forward to hear from you.

We like to build and develop businesses together with founders/management, but we are fine with hands-off approach.
Check Ventures for venture development.

WEB3: We do consider web3 project. But we are looking for disruptive project with 3Ts (see above). We will NOT consider any copycats.
