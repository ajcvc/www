Bullet Loan Program

AJC Lending

Summary: Attractive and secure loan at the wholesale rates

Suitable for:

  • Businesses with high EBITDA projects, in need of additional funds.
  • Investors and lenders, to hedge or divest their capital.
  • Ideal for infrastructure, factory, property, technology and similar deals
  • Mid to large projects: $40m minimal loan amount .

Other Benefits:

  • Simple start: Rapid feedback on initial project proposal
  • Non-amortised loan, no asset collateral needed.
  • Low-interest rates - versus the risk of the project
  • Accessible: with high lending multiples of Qualifying Capital
  • No cost occure before in-principle approval

NOT Suitable for:

  • Projects or plans that require immediate funding - due diligence and proof examination take reasonable time.
  • Projects or plans without healthy cash flow projections or clear use of funds

Loan Amount Minimum $40m up to $200m
Source of Capital USA
Loan Re-payments Non-amortised: Interests paid quarterly, principal at maturity
Allocation Worldwide (stability is one of the major criteria)
Loan Tenure 4 years (optional extensions)
Collateral Unsecured: No asset collateral required
Qualifying Capital Required, not lien (not pledged as collateral).
Compliance and AML required. Process is secured by trusted parties (banks, underwriters, custodians...).

First step: Submit a short brief/presentation of the projects for initial evaluation. You can include a basic plan on the use of loan, projections, and a business plan.
